About Trading Standards Approved

  • The media often highlights ‘rogue traders’ and the damage they can do, contrasting them with ‘Trusted Traders‘ who ensure reliable service and protect people’s property.
  • Trading Standards are committed to using our skills and knowledge to help residents make safer, more confident choices about who they do business with, ensuring they choose Trading Standards Approved businesses that adhere to the highest standards.
  • UKTSA supports the growth of businesses offering Trading Standards Approved Customer Services , ensuring residents can connect with trusted, reliable providers.
  • This site offers a curated list of trusted local tradespeople committed to fair and honest trading.
  • UKTSA offers a list of nearby companies committed to fair trading for a genuinely reliable experience. Each company on the list is a Trading Standards Approved Services, implying they have undergone screening and approval prior to joining.
  • This trading standards scheme currently operates in the counties that we serve currently are Buckinghamshire, Surrey and Worcestershire.
  • UKTSA is delivered through a partnership between Buckinghamshire, Surrey and Worcestershire Trading Standards and United Knowledge Ltd as a genuine partnership rather than outsourcing. This scheme blends the expertise of Trading Standards and United Knowledge Ltd to create a powerful platform which has the ability to grow over time and integrate additional benefits for businesses and residents.
