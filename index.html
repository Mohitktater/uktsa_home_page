Partnerships

Buckinghamshire

Trading Standards are responsible for regulating consumer protection and operates as a joint service with Buckinghamshire – supporting local business and protecting residents across both Local Government Areas.

Surrey

Trading Standards are responsible for regulating consumer protection and operates as a joint service with Buckinghamshire – supporting local business and protecting residents across both Local Government Areas.

Worcestershire

Trading Standards are responsible for regulating consumer protection. We operate as a joint service with Worcestershire – supporting local business and protecting residents across both Local Government Areas.

Independence CIC

Independence CIC collaborates with Trading Standards to link local companies with clients in need of reliable, qualified experts. Residents may feel more confident about the tradespeople they choose because to this relationship, which also helps businesses build their reputations.

United Knowledge Ltd

United Knowledge Ltd is part of a group of companies delivering services to businesses and Local Authorities which include Dynamic Purchasing Systems (DPS), vetting and pre-qualification checks for council approved lists, warranties and a route to TrustMark registration.
